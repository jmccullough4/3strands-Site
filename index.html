Our Story

Veteran Families, American Values

3 Strands Cattle Co. is owned and operated by two veteran families united by faith, service, and a passion for quality beef. We bring military discipline to every aspect of our operation while partnering with trusted ranchers across the Southeast who share our values.

As we've grown, we've expanded our network to source premium beef from American ranchers throughout the Southeast, ensuring consistent quality and supply for families across Florida. Every cut is traceable, every relationship is stewarded with integrity, and a portion of every sale supports local food banks and ministries.

Currently serving: Florida markets with delivery and pickup options.
