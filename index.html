From Florida Partner to Your Counter

Our distribution playbook keeps every Prime order moving with integrity, traceability, and prayerful care from sourcing through delivery.

  1. Source & Inspect: Reserve Florida herds and hogs, verify USDA grades, and confirm availability windows.
  2. Coordinate Processing: Align cut sheets with your specs and our partner butchers for consistent case-ready product.
  3. Stage & Store: Hold product in monitored cold storage, label by ranch and processor, and prep for your receiving team.
  4. Deliver & Support: Execute statewide delivery, share merchandising support, and tithe from every sale.
