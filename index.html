Our Story

Veteran Families, Florida Roots

3 Strands Cattle Co. is owned and operated by two veteran families united by faith, service, and a passion for quality beef. We bring military discipline to every aspect of our operation while partnering with multi-generation Florida ranchers who share our values.

Our mission is simple: glorify God through honest business, support local farmers, and provide your family with beef you can trust. Every cut is traceable, every relationship is stewarded with integrity, and a portion of every sale supports local food banks and ministries.

"A cord of three strands is not quickly broken." - Ecclesiastes 4:12

Faith-Driven

We pray over every partnership and operate with Biblical integrity in all we do.

Transparent

Know your rancher. We provide full traceability from pasture to package.

Community-First

We tithe from every sale and support local food banks and ministries.

Quality-Obsessed

Only Prime and Choice grades. We never compromise on what reaches your table.
