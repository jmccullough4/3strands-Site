Mission Statement

At 3 Strands Cattle Co., we are united in faith and purpose to glorify Jesus Christ through honest business practices, faithful stewardship of resources, and devoted service to others. Guided by Biblical principles of integrity, humility, and love for neighbor, we operate in a manner that honors God, supports local farmers, and provides quality beef, pork, and chicken to our community — always putting people over profit.

Pasture-Raised Offerings

Hand-selected cuts tailored for restaurants, families, and ministry partners who expect uncompromising quality.

Grass-Fed Beef

Sourced from Black Angus and Charolais cross herds, humanely raised on open pasture and finished on natural forage for rich marbling and flavor.

  • Prime steaks, roasts, and ground blends
  • Dry-aged and vacuum-sealed for freshness
  • Bulk quarters, halves, and whole beef shares

Heritage Pork

Outdoor-raised Berkshire and Duroc hogs with access to woodlands and non-GMO feed, delivering sweet, tender meat in every cut.

  • Pastured bacon, chops, ribs, and sausage
  • Smoked and fresh specialty selections
  • Whole hog packages for events and ministries

Cage-Free Chicken

Free-ranging broilers with mobile coops, scratch grains, and clean water to ensure juicy, nutrient-dense poultry for every kitchen.

  • Whole birds and custom-cut pieces
  • Bone broth kits and pasture-raised eggs
  • CSA shares with weekly delivery options

From Pasture to Provision

Our integrated process brings premium protein to your table with integrity, traceability, and prayerful care at every stage.

  1. Pasture Planning: Select forage rotations and partner farms aligned with regenerative standards.
  2. Animal Care: Daily welfare checks, holistic health plans, and stress-free handling.
  3. Harvest & Butchery: USDA-inspected processing with artisan butchers for precise cuts.
  4. Delivery & Ministry: Flexible delivery schedules, food ministry donations, and menu collaboration.
